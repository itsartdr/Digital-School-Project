Co-Founder

Hana Qerimi

Hana Qerimi is the Co-Founder and Senior Operating Officer at the Digital School. Hana completed her business management studies at UBT as well as her master studies in management consulting in London. Co-Founder

Darsej Rizaj

Darsej Rizaj is the Co-Founder and Executive Director of the Digital School. Darsej graduated in Communication and Technology Sciences from the South East European University in Macedonia. Darsej has worked in the ICT sector for more than 12 years. He has worked primarily as a Network Architect for the largest US and EMEA telecom providers such as AT&T, British Telecom, Vodafone, Orange, CradlePoint, Mobily, Tellabs, Cisco and Ciena.
